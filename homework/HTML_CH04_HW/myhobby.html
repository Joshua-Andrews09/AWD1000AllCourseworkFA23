Golf History

The same people who banned golf were also the ones who created the sport.
Scottish people invented golf in 1457. No one truly knows the earliest days of the sport.
The most accepted theory is the game was created during the high Middle Ages.
Even though golf was played publicly at first, people in the country created golf courses and clubs.
This is why both are still prevalent today.
