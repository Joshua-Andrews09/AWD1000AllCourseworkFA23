Pasta With Red Sauce

Pasta Ingredients

  • Pasta box
  • Red sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps To Make Pasta

  1. First turn on stove top burner and wait til a boil.
  2. Put pasta noodles in water.
  3. Take pasta out when noodles are cooked.
  4. After Pasta is cooked, strain the noodles over sink
  5. Now take a plate and put the amount of noodled you Want.
  6. Apply the red sauce to the pasta
  7. if you would like, add parmesan cheese.
